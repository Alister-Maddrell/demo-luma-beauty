Our Story

Beauty With
Intention

We founded Luma with a simple belief: luxury beauty should be thoughtful, inclusive, and kind — to your skin and to the planet.

The Beginning

Born From a Ritual

Luma began in a small Sydney studio in 2022, born from the belief that your skincare routine should be the most intentional part of your day. Our founder, after years in luxury beauty, saw a gap — products that performed brilliantly but felt generic, and brands that looked beautiful but didn't deliver.

We set out to create something different: formulations that marry cutting-edge science with the purest botanicals, wrapped in an experience that makes you pause, breathe, and reconnect with yourself.

What We Stand For

Our Values

01

Clean Science

Every formula is backed by clinical research and free from parabens, sulphates, synthetic fragrances, and phthalates. We believe in transparency — every ingredient is listed and every claim is substantiated.

02

Sustainable Luxury

Our packaging is 100% recyclable or refillable. We partner with carbon-neutral shipping providers and source ingredients from ethical, sustainable suppliers. Luxury doesn't have to cost the earth.

03

Inclusive Beauty

Beauty is for everyone. Our shade ranges are extensive, our formulas are tested on all skin types, and our brand celebrates the diversity that makes each of us unique and beautiful.

Our Ingredients

Only What
Your Skin Needs

We work with a team of cosmetic chemists and dermatologists to develop formulas that are effective at the lowest possible concentration. No filler ingredients, no marketing gimmicks — just what works.

Our hero ingredients include stabilised Vitamin C, bakuchiol (a plant-based retinol alternative), Australian kakadu plum, and ethically sourced rose hip from the Adelaide Hills.

Sustainability

Better for
the Planet

By 2027, we're committed to being a fully circular brand. Our refill programme launches this year, reducing packaging waste by up to 70%. Our glass bottles are designed to be beautiful enough to keep — and when you're ready, fully recyclable.

We offset 200% of our carbon emissions through partnerships with reforestation programmes in Australia and Southeast Asia.
